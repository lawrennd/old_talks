\newslide{Bayes' Rule Reminder}

$$
\text{posterior} =
\frac{\text{likelihood}\times\text{prior}}{\text{marginal likelihood}}
$$

Four components:

1. Prior distribution
2. Likelihood
3. Posterior distribution
4. Marginal likelihood
